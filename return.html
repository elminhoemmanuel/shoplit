FAQ's Timelines Refunds

Procedure

Login and go to ORDERS, then click return on the item to be returned

Select the quantity of the ordered item you wish to return and specify the reason for return.

Choose the desired refund method and pickup choice i.e dropoff or door pickup

Review your return information and submit return request.

Timelines

Easy return and refund

You can return all eligible item(s) within 7 days. Click here to see Frequently asked Questions.

Prepare the item

Place the item in its original packaging, including any accessories, tags, labels or freebies. If its an electronic device please ensure all passwords are removed, otherwise, your return will be invalid.

Drop off the items or request pickup

Return the item at our pickup stations or we can pick up from you within 1 - 4 business days. Always ensure your return slip is signed by our agents as your proof of return.

Refund Processed.

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process your refund within 10 business days into your registered account.
