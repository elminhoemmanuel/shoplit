How do i pay with my debit card?

Checkout

Proceed to checkout and select Pay now with card.

Select

On invoice page click on pay with paystack.

Details

On the secure page that comes up enter your card details:

  • Card numnber.
  • Expiry date.
  • CVV
click on complete order to finish. Please note depending on your bank you maybe asked to provide and OTP code sent to your registered phone number. Also Rossmart does not store your card details.

How do i pay with bank transfer?

Checkout

Proceed to checkout and select Pay with mobile or bank transfer.

Copy

On invoice page copy account number.

Transfer

Securely transfer the funds from your bank or bank app to the account number to complete payment.
